An AI-powered platform built to modernize the handicraft ecosystem with automated listings, trend intelligence, and language-free buyer–seller communication. 

•AI Listing Generator: Built an AI Listing Generator to auto-create product titles, descriptions, tags, and price suggestions from uploaded images.
•AI Trend Spotter Engine: Developed an AI Trend Spotter to identify high-demand categories and seasonal market opportunities for artisans.
•Artist & Workshop Profiles (Instagram-style): Designed social-style profiles for artisans and workshops to showcase products, stories, reels, and customer engagement, improving discoverability and brand building.
•Multilingual Buyer–Artist Messenger: Implemented a multilingual buyer–seller chat system with real-time translation (English ↔ local languages).
